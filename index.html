How We Operate

Our Misson

PCs for Kids is a self-funded registered charity, staffed entirely by volunteers. We donate computers to children who are in personal or family circumstances which prevent them having the required access to a suitable computer. The child must be nominated by a relevant support organisation. Maintaining a stock of computers suitable for providing to children in need is the highest priority.
